Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Braintrain. This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information and we do not create or store accounts.

Login is required to provide live interaction only with the neural network.

>

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Information: Information about how you use our app, such as your activity within the app and the values you enter within the nodes.
  • Node Values: The text you enter within the nodes is presented live to all who log in. All values stored in the nodes are deleted routinely.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our app, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Service: To operate and maintain our app, and to develop new features and functionality.
  • To Ensure Security: To protect the security of our app and your information.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you have given us your explicit consent to share your information.
  • For Legal Reasons: When we are required to do so by law or in response to a legal request.
  • With Service Providers: With third parties that perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer service.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no security measures are completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.
